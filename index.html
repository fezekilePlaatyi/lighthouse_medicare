About Us

At Lighthouse Medicare, we are dedicated to providing exceptional, comprehensive care for women across all stages of life. Founded by Dr. Maria Dena, a leading Obstetrician and Gynaecologist and Senior Consultant with credentials in Medical Law, Ethics, and Reproductive Biotechnology, our team specializes in maternity care, fertility services, gynecology, and menopause management. Dr. Dena, along with the future expansion of our consulting team, is committed to delivering the highest standard of care.

Since our establishment, we have prioritized creating a warm and supportive environment, empowering women to make informed health decisions with confidence. Our commitment to women’s health is unwavering, and our services are designed to address each unique need with personalized attention and compassion. Whether it’s routine gynecological care, advanced fertility treatments, or dedicated support through pregnancy, our goal is to support you every step of the way. Your health journey is important to us, and we are here to provide the expert guidance you deserve for a lifetime of wellness.

Our Services

Pregnancy Care

Our pregnancy care program provides support from conception to delivery, offering expert prenatal care and ultrasound services to monitor your baby's health and growth.

Gynecological Services

Our services include gynecological surgeries, ultrasound screenings, Pap smears, and breast health assessments, focusing on personalized and compassionate care.

Menopause Care

We offer support for women navigating menopause, including symptom management, hormone therapy, and ultrasound assessments to ensure your well-being.

Your Trusted OBGYN Specialists

We aim to be a reliable partner in women's health by providing compassionate and specialized care at every stage. Our dedicated team focuses on addressing women's unique health needs with personalized fertility, gynecological, and menopause services.

We strive to foster a welcoming environment where every woman feels empowered and supported. Through expert guidance and individualized care, we aim to instill confidence and ensure women have the resources to make informed health decisions.
